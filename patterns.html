The economy of Smith County

This treemap shows Smith County's economy divided into the major groupings of the North American Industry Classification System. Each box is scaled relative to the amount of the economy it represents, with the total area being 100% of the economy.

All data is taken from the U.S. Census Bureau's recently released 2009 County Business Patterns survey.
