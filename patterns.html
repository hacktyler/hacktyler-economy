The economy of Smith County

This graph, called a treemap, shows Smith County's economy divided into the major groupings of the North American Industry Classification System. Each box is scaled relative to the proportion of the business activity it represents.

Data for this application is taken from the U.S. Census Bureau's recently released 2009 County Business Patterns survey.
